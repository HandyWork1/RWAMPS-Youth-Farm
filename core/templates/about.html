About Us

Rwamps Youths farmers Groups Limited (RYFA-GROUP) is a dynamic, state-of-the art, quality driven private agribusiness company based in Uganda-Kampala. We aim to be a global leader of choice by providing premium modern contract farming techniques. Focusing on increasing production, value addition, market leakage, commercialisation and innovation for exclusive growth and sustainable development through modernisation and mechanisation while forging a better future for local farmers. We produce all year round products, conform to internationally recognised food and safety standards; assuring customers of quality, traceability, reliability, and service delivery.
